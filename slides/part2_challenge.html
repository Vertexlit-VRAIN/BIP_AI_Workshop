Round 3 — Debrief
"Why do these failures happen?"

Pattern matching

AI was trained on text patterns, not facts. It produces what sounds right, not what is right.

Sycophancy

AI tends to agree with the user, even when the user is wrong. It was rewarded for being helpful, not for pushing back.

No understanding

AI has no model of truth or reality. It cannot verify its own output. That is your job.

Key lesson: AI is not a source of truth. Domain expertise is what catches AI mistakes. Always verify.
